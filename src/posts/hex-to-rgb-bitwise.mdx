A color is usually represented by a hexadecimal number. For example,
the color <ColoredText color="#FF0000">#FF0000</ColoredText> is red.
The hexadecimal number is made up of six digits. The first two digits
represent the red component, the next two digits represent the green
component, and the last two digits represent the blue component.
[See this wikipedia article for more information](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hex_color).
